Dependencies for OpenBroadcaster Server.  This list may be incomplete.

Check for updated installation info at http://wiki.openbroadcaster.com 

Dependencies are Debian/Ubuntu package names.

- libav-tools (version 9 or later)
- php5-mysql
- php5-gd
- php5-curl
- php5-imagick
- vorbis-tools
- festival
- imagemagick

Codec dependencies for transcoding, etc:

- libavcodec-extra-53
- libavdevice-extra-53
- libavfilter-extra-2
- libavformat-extra-53
- libavutil-extra-51
- libpostproc-extra-52
- libswscale-extra-2
